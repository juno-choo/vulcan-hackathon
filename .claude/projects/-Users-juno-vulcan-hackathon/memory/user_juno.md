Juno is a developer at a hackathon building Vulcan ("Airbnb for Skilled Hands"). Has a teammate. They bonded over how companies solved pre-AI era problems — particularly the AWS origin story (Amazon renting idle servers post-Christmas). Applied the same lens to physical tools: a typical lawn mower sits idle 99.7% of its life (20 hours used out of 8,760 available). This idle-capacity insight drove the Vulcan concept.
